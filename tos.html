✦ GENERAL

  • By commissioning me, you agree to all terms listed below.
  • I reserve the right to decline any commission request for any reason.
  • Prices are based on base prices as seen in my pricelist. Additional elements, complex designs, or special requests may increase the final price.
  • I reserve the right to post the finished artwork on my portofolio or social media unless the client specifically requests that the artwork remain private.
  • I do NOT allow my artwork to be used in AI-generated imagery or any blockchain/NFT related projects.

✦ COMMUNICATION

Communication for commissions can be done through:

bbon bonna0bon BONNA0BON (X) SEND EMAIL

Clients are expected to:

  • Provide clear references for characters or designs.
  • Clearly explain your request.
  • Respond to questions or updates within a reasonable time.
  • If there is no response from client for 7 days, the commission may be paused until communication resumes.

✦ PAYMENT

  • Accepted payment methods: PayPal, Ko-fi, Dana, Gopay.
  • Payment options: Full Upfront payment OR 50% deposit + 50% After completion.
  • Work will not begin until payment or deposit has been received. The final artwork will be delivered after the full payment is completed.
  • Refunds are depending on what stage the commission is at. No refund is granted after the sketch phase is approved.

✦ REVISIONS

  • Minor revisions (small adjustments such as small details or take less than 15 mins) are free and unlimited.
  • Major revisions (pose changes, etc. after sketch is approved) may incur extra fees depending on the progress of the artwork.
  • Major revisions should preferably be requested during the sketch stage.

✦ CANCELLATION & REFUNDS

  • The client may cancel the commission before the sketch is started.
  • Any upfront money paid will be fully refunded if canceled before sketching.
  • If the client cancels after the sketch has been started and payment has been made, 70% of the payment will be refunded. 30 % will be retained to compensate for the work already done.
  • If the artwork is already significantly in progress, no refund will be provided.
  • I also reserve the right to cancel a commission. Refunds in such cases will be provided.

✦ TURNAROUND TIME

  • The turnaround time is 3-14 days per commission, depending on complexity, queue and my personal schedule.
  • Please be patient and do not rush the artist.

✦ USAGE RIGHTS

  • Personal Use: Use the artwork as a profile picture, wallpaper, inside non-profit projects. You must give proper credit. You cannot use it for merchandise.
  • Commercial Use: Requires an extra fee (x2 regular price). This allows the artwork to be used for branding, streaming assets, merchandise, etc.
  • I (the artist) retain the copyright of the artwork and the right to post the commissioned artwork on my portfolio/socials unless agreed otherwise.

AGREEMENT

By commissioning me, the client confirms that they have read and agreed to these Terms of Service.
